1. Introduction – What’s your opinion?

Case Study

In January 2026, the US army entered Venezuela in a major operation. The United States attacked and captured President Nicolás Maduro and his wife. The U.S. government said the action was necessary for security and justice reasons. However, most experts and countries say the operation broke international law.

What do you think about this operation? Is it fair? Is it good for the people of Venezuela? Is it dangerous?

Feedback

International laws and international organizations exist to stop wars. There are more than 200 countries in the world and the people living in them have very different opinions. Most of these opinions are valid, and we should always try to listen and respect other culture's choices, as long as they do not disrespect our own choices. Wars kill innocent people, and they are usually started because of money, and nothing else.

Stop and think

During a war, two or more parts fight each other. Each part will try to convince you that they are right and the other one is wrong.

Feedback

The two participants of a conflict may have valid reasons to be angry. You should always think for yourself after listening to both parts. It's best if you investigate and search for evidence.

Can you look for information?

Search for information on the internet. Answer the following questions in order.
Why do some people hate Nicolás Maduro?
According to the US president, Donald Trump, why did they capture Maduro?
What is "international law"?
What does the president of Russia, Vladimir Putin, think about this operation?
