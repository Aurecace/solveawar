Listen & think

Activity - Listen to the song and aswer the questions

  1. According to the song, you should practice what you preach. This means that you do what you say. For example, your mom tells you "clean and arrange your bedroom" because she cleans and arranges her bedroom. What do you think? Do you practice what you preach to your friends and family?
  2. Look at the following verbs from the song. Make a simple sentence with each one:
    • to respect
    • to kill
    • to care about
  3. Look in the internet for the meaning of to turn the other cheek. Can you think of a similar expression in Spanish? Do you know someone that turns the other cheek when there is a conflict?
