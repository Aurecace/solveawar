2. Let’s build – Grammar & strategies

Do I really need grammar?

Ah, yes, grammar. That boring thing that we have to study in order to pass the test... do we really need it to learn English?

The main objective of learning English, as with any other language, is to be able to communicate with people that can't speak Spanish. Grammar is the book of rules that we follow to use a language correctly. Can we really speak a language if we don't follow its rules?

Case Study

You are hanging out with a new American friend that came to Spain to study this year at your school. She gives you her phone and says "look at the post of this guy! I can't believe it...".

You look at her phone. It's a video of an angry man shouting. He says horrible things about his experience. He thinks that Spain is a dangerous country where you can't go outside alone. He believes that people should be allowed to carry weapons. He is absolutely wrong! Your city is perfectly safe and his solution is terrible!

Think and answer

To convince someone that Spain is safe, you can share with your American friend the real experience of people that you know (your parents, your teachers, your friends...). To agree or disagree with a solution, you can discuss the possible consequences.

  • Can you share other people's opinion in English?
  • Will you understand your American friend if she speaks about her family's opinion?
  • Can you speak about consequences of a possible solution in English?

Feedback

  • In English, we use something called reported speech to speak about other people's opinions and experiences. It's very similar to Spanish "estilo indirecto".
  • When we want to speak about possible future consequences of something, we can use a tool called second conditional.

In this unit, we will learn how to understand and use these two tools.

Think about it...

By learning new grammar strategies, you can explain things clearly and precisely. Could you explain things to your American friend without those strategies?

Feedback

You can communicate without learning all the strategies in the world. There are many ways to say the same thing. However, if you learn new strategies, you will be able to say more things. If you use few words and sentences, the other person might not understand what you meant. She might even understand the opposite idea!
