Final task

Final task – Debate with your teacher

For this final task, you will simulate a debate with your teacher. Think of, at least, 3 solutions that you want to apply in our English classroom.

1st part

  • Record a short video (2 to 4 minutes) where you, as the student, present the conflicts, the solutions that you have thought, and the arguments that support those solutions.

  • You will get bonus points if you...

    • Use studies or articles to support your point.

    • Use indirect speech to share relatives’ or friends’ opinions that agree with you.

2nd part

  • Record a short answer (2 to 4 minutes) where you act as the teacher. Try to understand the points of view of a teacher in your class!
  • You can answer showing agreement or disagreement but you must propose a different solution and at least one counterargument.
  • You will get bonus points if you...
    • Use the second conditional to counter the ideas of your student more than 2 times.
