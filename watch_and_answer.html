Watch and answer

Should I speak out?

How many times have you come across a post that outrages you? A girl insulting your favorite artist, a football fan saying your team is the worst and never wins, a politician lying to your face...

What do you do when you see those?

Some people only want to be controversial because that makes them famous. Some people might not understand that they are wrong, even when you give them clear evidence.

But most of the people will think about something, and sometimes even change their opinion, if you respectfully correct them. Be polite and use reliable sources, and you will be surprised!

But remember! In social media, there are many AI bots nowadays that only want to be controversial to become viral. It's useless to speak to someone that doesn't listen to you.
