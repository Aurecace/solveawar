The end

Text

Congratulations! You have reached the end of this unit.

You now can use your English to understand, mediate and solve conflicts. Thank you for trying your best!

Remember that everyone has a reason behind an opinion. And that listening and debating should always be the first way to arrive to a solution. Words don't kill innocent people. Wars and violence do. Let's try to avoid them while we can.

Think about it...

Have you switched your point of view about something? Have you learned new things about a conflict that you thought you knew all about? Is it easier to find information when you know more than one language?
